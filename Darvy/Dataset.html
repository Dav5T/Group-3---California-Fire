Dataset

  • Location: State of California

  • Year: 2018 - 2023

  • Temperature: Used the average temperature between July-August since those are hottest times of the year

  • Droughts: Droughts (D4) the two 2 timeperiods were Dec. 2020 and May 2021

  • Causes of fires 2018-2022 (2023 not available): Data includes causes, acres burned, location

  • Insects: Three species of insect deemed most destructive were the Gold Spotted Oak Borer, the Invasive Shot Hole Borer, and the Mountain Pine Beetle
